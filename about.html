About Just Dance Party

Welcome to Just Dance Party, a community-driven project dedicated to bringing back the joy of Just Dance Unlimited!

Ubisoft's decision to discontinue Just Dance Unlimited left many fans disappointed. We believe that the music and the fun should live on. Our mod aims to restore access to the vast library of songs and features that were once available, allowing players to continue enjoying their favorite dance routines.

Our Mission

  • To preserve the legacy of Just Dance Unlimited.
  • To provide a platform for the community to access and enjoy unlimited songs.
  • To foster a vibrant and active Just Dance modding community.
