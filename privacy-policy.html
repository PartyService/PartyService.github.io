Privacy Policy

At Just Dance Party, we are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our unwavering approach to handling any information related to your use of our unofficial Just Dance Party modification ("Mod"). Our core principle is **privacy-first**, ensuring that your dance experience is entirely focused on fun, without concerns about your personal information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We unequivocally state that Just Dance Party does **not** collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.
  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address or unique device identifiers.
  • Your location data.
  • Any demographic information (age, gender, etc.).
  • Usage data such as playtime, songs played, scores, or preferences.
  • Information about your device (e.g., operating system, hardware specifications).

We do not employ analytics tools, tracking cookies, web beacons, or any similar technologies designed to monitor or collect information about your activities within the Mod or on this website. Our design philosophy ensures that your interaction with Just Dance Party remains entirely private and anonymous.

2. How We Use Information (Or Don't)

Since we do not collect any personal data, we have no information to use for any purpose. This means:

  • We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or promotional activities.
  • We do not create user profiles or perform data analysis based on your usage.
  • Your activities within Just Dance Party, including your personalized playlists and themes, remain solely on our server but we don't access them.

Our commitment is to provide a purely self-contained experience where your privacy is inherently protected by design.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no information for us to share, sell, rent, or disclose to any third parties. Your privacy is paramount, and we will never compromise it by sharing non-existent data.

The only theoretical exception would be if we are compelled by a valid legal order, such as a court order or subpoena. However, since we hold no personal data, there would be no data to provide in such an event.

4. Data Security

While we do not collect or store user data, we still prioritize the overall security and integrity of our platform. We implement robust security measures in the development and distribution of the Just Dance Party Mod to ensure that the environment you interact with is as safe and secure as possible. Our aim is to provide a self-contained and trusted ecosystem for all users.

5. Third-Party Links

This website and the Just Dance Party Mod may contain links to other websites or services (e.g., Discord, Twitter, YouTube). Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by Just Dance Party.

6. Children's Privacy

Just Dance Party is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at jdpartyera@gmail.com so that we can address your concerns.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the bottom of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to contact us at:
Email: jdpartyera@gmail.com
